# Global AI Readiness and Technological Leadership Across Countries

This project analyzes global Artificial Intelligence (AI) readiness across countries using data visualization techniques in Python. The goal of the project is to explore how different countries compare in terms of AI capability and identify the factors that contribute to technological leadership.

The analysis uses the AI Index dataset and focuses on indicators such as talent, infrastructure, research capability, development activity, government strategy, and commercial AI adoption.

---

## Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- Identify the countries leading in global AI readiness
- Analyze how AI capability varies across regions
- Explore relationships between different AI indicators
- Visualize global AI development patterns using multiple visualization techniques

---

## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is the **AI Index dataset** obtained from Kaggle.

Dataset characteristics:

- 62 countries
- 13 variables
- Includes both numerical and categorical indicators

Key indicators in the dataset:

- Talent
- Infrastructure
- Operating Environment
- Research
- Development
- Government Strategy
- Commercial
- Total AI Score

Dataset Source:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/katerynameleshenko/ai-index

1. Distribution of Global AI Readiness Scores  
2. Top 10 Countries in AI Readiness

4. Correlation Heatmap of AI Indicators  
5. Global AI Readiness Choropleth Map  
6. Network Graph of AI Indicators  

These visualizations help illustrate patterns in AI development and highlight disparities in technological capability across countries.

---

## Key Insights

Some of the key findings from the analysis include:

- AI readiness is concentrated among a small group of technologically advanced countries.
- The United States is the leading country in AI readiness.
- Europe and Asia-Pacific regions contain several strong AI economies.
- Research capability shows a strong relationship with overall AI readiness.
- AI development depends on an interconnected ecosystem including talent, infrastructure, and commercial activity.
